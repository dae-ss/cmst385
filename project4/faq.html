What is the Legion of Honor?

The Legion of Honor is an art museum in San Francisco, renowned for its impressive collection of European art spanning from ancient times to the early 20th century.

What kind of art does the Legion of Honor feature?

The Legion of Honor features a rich collection of European art, including paintings, sculptures, decorative arts, and more. The collection spans various periods, offering a comprehensive overview of artistic developments from ancient civilizations to the early 20th century.
